Support

Select your app to find answers and troubleshooting steps.

Anime data is not loading

If Anime Pulse is not loading anime information, try the following steps:

  • Check that your internet connection is active.
  • Pull down on the main screen to refresh the data.
  • Close Anime Pulse fully and reopen it.
  • Confirm that the AniList service is reachable by visiting AniList in a browser on the same network.
  • If you are offline, cached data from your last successful connection may still appear.

Images are not loading

Anime cover images are loaded from external servers. If images are not appearing:

  • Check that your internet connection is active.
  • If images remain broken after reconnecting, try clearing the app cache: go to Android Settings → Apps → Anime Pulse → Storage → Clear cache.
  • Avoid clearing App data unless necessary, as this will remove your favourites and preferences.

Episode notifications are not arriving

If you are not receiving episode reminder notifications, check all of the following:

  • Ensure Anime Pulse has notification permission granted in Android Settings → Apps → Anime Pulse → Notifications.
  • Ensure the Episode Reminders notification channel is enabled within those settings.
  • Check that your device's battery optimisation or power-saving mode is not preventing Anime Pulse from scheduling background tasks. You may need to disable battery restrictions for Anime Pulse specifically.
  • Confirm that the anime title you expect a reminder for has a confirmed future airing date and time. If the airing schedule is not yet confirmed by the data provider, a reminder cannot be scheduled.
  • Confirm that episode reminders are enabled in Anime Pulse's own settings screen.

My Week does not show all my favourites

My Week only displays favourite anime that have a confirmed episode scheduled to air during the selected week. If a series you have saved as a favourite is not appearing in My Week, it may be because:

  • The series has finished airing and has no upcoming episodes.
  • The next episode airing date has not yet been confirmed by the data provider.
  • The episode falls outside the currently selected week.

Anime with a confirmed future airing date will appear automatically in the correct week once the schedule is published.

How to delete local data

All data stored by Anime Pulse is kept locally on your device. To remove it:

  • Clear app data: go to Android Settings → Apps → Anime Pulse → Storage → Clear data. This removes your favourites, episode status, preferences and cached content.
  • Uninstall the app: uninstalling Anime Pulse removes all locally stored data from your device.

Mini Forge Dev does not store your personal data on external servers, so no remote deletion request is required.

How do I unlock themes?

Themes in Letter Rise are unlocked by watching optional rewarded video ads. When a locked theme is shown, tap the unlock button and watch the ad to the end to unlock it permanently on your device.

Rewarded ads require an active internet connection. If no ad is available, try again after a short wait — ad availability can vary by region and time.

Rewarded or interstitial ads are not appearing

Letter Rise uses Google AdMob to serve ads. If ads are not loading:

  • Check that your internet connection is active.
  • Some ad types may not be available in your region at all times. This is normal and controlled by ad networks, not by the app.
  • For rewarded ads, wait a short time and try again — ad fill rates can vary.
  • Restarting the app may help refresh the ad connection.

The game is not saving my progress

Letter Rise saves all game progress — including high score, unlocked themes, and settings — locally on your device. If progress appears lost:

  • Check that you have not recently cleared Letter Rise's app storage data in Android Settings.
  • Uninstalling and reinstalling the app will remove all locally saved data, as it is not backed up to a cloud server.

How to delete saved data

All data stored by Letter Rise is kept locally on your device. To remove it:

  • Clear app data: go to Android Settings → Apps → Letter Rise → Storage → Clear data. This removes all saved progress, settings, and unlocked themes.
  • Uninstall the app: uninstalling Letter Rise removes all locally stored data from your device.

Sephylon does not store your game data on external servers, so no remote deletion request is required.
