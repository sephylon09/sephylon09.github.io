1. Overview

Anime Pulse is an Android application developed and published by Mini Forge Dev. This privacy policy explains what information is stored on your device, what data third-party services may process, and what choices you have regarding your privacy.

Anime Pulse is designed to minimise data collection. Where third-party services are used, they are disclosed here along with their purpose.

2. Information stored locally

Anime Pulse stores the following types of data locally on your device only. This information is not transmitted to Mini Forge Dev servers.

  • Your saved favourite anime titles
  • Watched episode status you have marked
  • Episode reminder preferences and notification settings
  • Your preferred title language (English, Romaji or Native)
  • Cached anime details and airing schedules for offline viewing

Uninstalling Anime Pulse or clearing application data through Android Settings removes all locally stored data from your device.

3. Anime data provider

Anime Pulse obtains anime information — including titles, descriptions, episode schedules, cover images and related metadata — from AniList or other clearly disclosed data sources. When the app fetches this information, your device makes a network request to the data provider's servers. The data provider's own privacy policy governs how they handle those requests.

Anime Pulse is not endorsed by or affiliated with AniList.

The AniList service and its API are subject to their own terms of use and privacy policy, available at the AniList website.

4. Advertising and Google AdMob

Anime Pulse may display advertisements served by Google AdMob, a mobile advertising platform operated by Google LLC. Including AdMob means the Google Mobile Ads SDK may be present in the application.

The Google Mobile Ads SDK may process certain information in order to serve advertisements and measure their performance. This may include, but is not necessarily limited to:

  • Device or advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App interaction and usage data
  • Diagnostic and crash information
  • Approximate location where applicable and permitted
  • Advertising and consent information

Google's privacy policy and advertising policies govern how Google processes information through AdMob. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Mini Forge Dev does not publish AdMob ad unit identifiers on this website. The publisher identifier used in app-ads.txt is disclosed separately for ad network authorisation purposes.

5. Consent and privacy choices

Anime Pulse may use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request advertising consent and present privacy choices where legally required, such as in regions covered by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and related regulations.

If consent is requested when you open the app, you will be presented with clear options before any personalised advertising is shown. You may be able to revisit your consent choices through the app's settings or privacy options menu.

Where consent is not legally required, standard advertising practices may apply in accordance with Google's policies.

6. Notifications

Anime Pulse can send optional episode reminder notifications to alert you when a favourite anime episode is scheduled to air. This feature is entirely optional.

Episode reminders can be enabled or disabled at any time from within the Anime Pulse settings screen. You can also manage notification permissions for Anime Pulse through your device's Android notification settings.

Reminders are scheduled locally on your device using Android's notification scheduling system. Mini Forge Dev does not use a push notification server to send episode alerts.

7. External links

Anime Pulse may contain links to external websites, anime streaming services or other third-party resources. When you follow an external link, you leave Anime Pulse and the linked website's own privacy policy applies.

Mini Forge Dev is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites.

8. Children and target audience

Anime Pulse is a general-audience application. It is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. Users in regulated jurisdictions should be aware of the applicable age-related requirements of their local laws regarding data privacy.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us at the address below so that we can take appropriate action.

9. Data retention

Data stored locally on your device (such as favourites, episode status and preferences) is retained until you clear the application data or uninstall Anime Pulse. Mini Forge Dev does not retain personal data on its own servers.

Third-party services such as Google AdMob may retain data in accordance with their own retention policies.

10. Data deletion

To delete all locally stored Anime Pulse data from your device, you can:

  • Go to Android Settings → Apps → Anime Pulse → Storage → Clear data. This removes all locally stored app data including favourites and preferences.
  • Uninstall Anime Pulse from your device. All locally stored data is removed when the app is uninstalled.

For data held by third-party services, please refer to those services' own data deletion instructions or contact them directly.

11. Security

Anime Pulse stores your data locally on your device and relies on Android's built-in application sandboxing to protect it from access by other apps. Mini Forge Dev takes reasonable care in developing the application, but no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Keeping your Android device updated and using appropriate device security features helps protect locally stored data.

12. Policy changes

This privacy policy may be updated when Anime Pulse adds or removes features, changes data handling practices, or integrates new third-party SDKs. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the date of the most recent revision.

Significant changes will be noted in the Anime Pulse app update release notes where practical.

13. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests or any concerns regarding this policy, please contact Mini Forge Dev by email:

miniforgedev@gmail.com

1. Overview

Letter Rise is a mobile word puzzle game developed and published by Sephylon (com.sephylon.letterrise). This privacy policy explains what information is stored on your device and what data third-party services may process when you use the app.

2. Information stored locally

Letter Rise may store the following data locally on your device. This information is not uploaded to a Letter Rise server.

  • Saved game progress
  • App settings and preferences
  • Selected theme and unlocked themes
  • Tutorial completion status
  • High score
  • Hint and theme-related preferences

This local game data is stored on the device and is not uploaded to a Letter Rise server.

3. Advertising and Google AdMob

Letter Rise uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. Ad types may include:

  • Rewarded ads, which you can optionally watch to unlock themes
  • Interstitial ads, which may appear occasionally during New Game transitions

The Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect or process data in order to serve advertisements and measure their performance. This may include, but is not necessarily limited to:

  • Device identifiers and advertising ID (such as the Android Advertising ID)
  • App interaction and usage data
  • Diagnostics and crash information
  • Approximate location where applicable and permitted
  • Other data according to Google's policies

Google's privacy policy governs how Google processes information through AdMob. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. No account required

Letter Rise does not require account registration or login. You can play the game without providing any personal information to Sephylon.

5. No in-app purchases

Letter Rise does not currently offer in-app purchases. Themes can be unlocked by watching optional rewarded ads.

6. Data sharing

Sephylon does not sell personal data. Letter Rise does not operate its own backend server for saved gameplay data — all game data is stored locally on your device.

Third-party services such as Google AdMob may process data in accordance with their own policies. Sephylon does not control how Google processes data through the AdMob SDK.

7. Children's privacy

Letter Rise is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. Because the app uses Google AdMob, which may collect advertising-related data, it is not designed as a children's app under COPPA or similar regulations.

8. Data deletion

Because Letter Rise stores game data locally on your device, you can delete it by:

  • Going to Android Settings → Apps → Letter Rise → Storage → Clear data. This removes all locally stored game data including progress, settings, and unlocked themes.
  • Uninstalling Letter Rise from your device. All locally stored data is removed when the app is uninstalled.

For data held by third-party services such as Google AdMob, please refer to those services' own data deletion instructions or contact them directly.

9. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated if Letter Rise's features change, especially if ads, analytics, online services, or in-app purchases are added or modified. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the date of the most recent revision.

1. Overview

LetterForge (com.sephylon.letterforge) is an Android word puzzle application developed and published by Mini Forge Dev. This privacy policy explains what information is stored on your device, what data third-party services may process, and what choices you have regarding your privacy.

LetterForge is designed to minimise data collection. Your gameplay data stays on your device. Where third-party services are used, they are disclosed here along with their purpose.

2. Offline gameplay and no account

LetterForge does not require an account, registration, or login of any kind. You can play all game modes, complete daily challenges, earn coins and XP, and track your progress without providing any personal information to Mini Forge Dev.

The core gameplay is available offline. LetterForge does not provide cloud sync. Your progress is stored locally on your device and is not backed up to a remote server operated by Mini Forge Dev.

3. Information stored locally

LetterForge may store the following types of data locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to Mini Forge Dev servers.

  • Game progress and current puzzle state
  • Daily challenge completion status
  • Coins and XP totals
  • Achievements and statistics
  • App settings and preferences
  • Game history

All locally stored data can be removed by clearing app data through Android Settings or by uninstalling LetterForge. See the Data deletion section for instructions.

4. Advertising and Google AdMob

LetterForge uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. Ad types include:

  • Rewarded ads — optional ads you may choose to watch in order to earn hints or other in-game assistance. The reward is granted only after the ad reward is earned.
  • Interstitial ads — full-screen ads that may appear at natural transition points during gameplay.

The Google Mobile Ads SDK may process certain information in order to serve advertisements and measure their performance. This may include, but is not necessarily limited to:

  • Device identifiers and advertising ID (such as the Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location derived from IP
  • App interaction and usage data
  • Diagnostics, crash, and performance information
  • Advertising and consent information

These advertising-related data types may be used by Google for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and SDK performance monitoring. Data is transmitted securely where the AdMob SDK communicates with Google.

Google's privacy policy governs how Google processes information through AdMob. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Consent and privacy choices

LetterForge may use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request advertising consent and present privacy choices where legally required, such as in regions covered by GDPR, CCPA, and related regulations. You may also manage advertising preferences through your Android device settings.

6. What the app does not access

LetterForge does not request and does not use precise location, contacts, camera, microphone, SMS, call logs, or payment information. The app does not collect or sell gameplay history to third parties.

7. Data sharing

Mini Forge Dev does not sell personal data. LetterForge does not operate its own backend server for saved gameplay data. Third-party services such as Google AdMob may process data in accordance with their own policies.

8. Children and target audience

LetterForge is a general-audience application. It is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. Because the app uses Google AdMob, which may collect advertising-related data, it is not designed as a children's app under COPPA or similar regulations.

9. Data deletion

To delete all locally stored LetterForge data from your device, you can:

  • Go to Android Settings → Apps → LetterForge → Storage → Clear data. This removes all locally stored app data including progress, coins, XP, achievements, and settings.
  • Uninstall LetterForge from your device. All locally stored data is removed when the app is uninstalled.

10. Security

LetterForge stores your data locally and relies on Android's built-in application sandboxing to protect it. Mini Forge Dev takes reasonable care in developing the application, but no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

11. Policy changes

This privacy policy may be updated when LetterForge adds or removes features, changes data-handling practices, or integrates new third-party SDKs. The "Last updated" date at the top of this panel reflects the date of the most recent revision.
