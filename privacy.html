1. Overview

AnimePulse is an Android application developed and published by Mini Forge Dev. This privacy policy explains what information is stored on your device, what data third-party services may process, and what choices you have regarding your privacy.

AnimePulse is designed to minimise data collection. Where third-party services are used, they are disclosed here along with their purpose.

2. Information stored locally

AnimePulse stores the following types of data locally on your device only. This information is not transmitted to Mini Forge Dev servers.

  • Your saved favourite anime titles
  • Watched episode status you have marked
  • Episode reminder preferences and notification settings
  • Your preferred title language (English, Romaji or Native)
  • Cached anime details and airing schedules for offline viewing

Uninstalling AnimePulse or clearing application data through Android Settings removes all locally stored data from your device.

3. Anime data provider

AnimePulse obtains anime information — including titles, descriptions, episode schedules, cover images and related metadata — from AniList or other clearly disclosed data sources. When the app fetches this information, your device makes a network request to the data provider's servers. The data provider's own privacy policy governs how they handle those requests.

AnimePulse is not endorsed by or affiliated with AniList.

The AniList service and its API are subject to their own terms of use and privacy policy, available at the AniList website.

4. Advertising and Google AdMob

AnimePulse may display advertisements served by Google AdMob, a mobile advertising platform operated by Google LLC. Including AdMob means the Google Mobile Ads SDK may be present in the application.

The Google Mobile Ads SDK may process certain information in order to serve advertisements and measure their performance. This may include, but is not necessarily limited to:

  • Device or advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App interaction and usage data
  • Diagnostic and crash information
  • Approximate location where applicable and permitted
  • Advertising and consent information

Google's privacy policy and advertising policies govern how Google processes information through AdMob. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Mini Forge Dev does not publish AdMob ad unit identifiers on this website. The publisher identifier used in app-ads.txt is disclosed separately for ad network authorisation purposes.

5. Consent and privacy choices

AnimePulse may use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request advertising consent and present privacy choices where legally required, such as in regions covered by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and related regulations.

If consent is requested when you open the app, you will be presented with clear options before any personalised advertising is shown. You may be able to revisit your consent choices through the app's settings or privacy options menu.

Where consent is not legally required, standard advertising practices may apply in accordance with Google's policies.

6. Notifications

AnimePulse can send optional episode reminder notifications to alert you when a favourite anime episode is scheduled to air. This feature is entirely optional.

Episode reminders can be enabled or disabled at any time from within the AnimePulse settings screen. You can also manage notification permissions for AnimePulse through your device's Android notification settings.

Reminders are scheduled locally on your device using Android's notification scheduling system. Mini Forge Dev does not use a push notification server to send episode alerts.

7. External links

AnimePulse may contain links to external websites, anime streaming services or other third-party resources. When you follow an external link, you leave AnimePulse and the linked website's own privacy policy applies.

Mini Forge Dev is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites.

8. Children and target audience

AnimePulse is a general-audience application. It is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. Users in regulated jurisdictions should be aware of the applicable age-related requirements of their local laws regarding data privacy.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us at the address below so that we can take appropriate action.

9. Data retention

Data stored locally on your device (such as favourites, episode status and preferences) is retained until you clear the application data or uninstall AnimePulse. Mini Forge Dev does not retain personal data on its own servers.

Third-party services such as Google AdMob may retain data in accordance with their own retention policies.

10. Data deletion

To delete all locally stored AnimePulse data from your device, you can:

  • Go to Android Settings → Apps → AnimePulse → Storage → Clear data. This removes all locally stored app data including favourites and preferences.
  • Uninstall AnimePulse from your device. All locally stored data is removed when the app is uninstalled.

For data held by third-party services, please refer to those services' own data deletion instructions or contact them directly.

11. Security

AnimePulse stores your data locally on your device and relies on Android's built-in application sandboxing to protect it from access by other apps. Mini Forge Dev takes reasonable care in developing the application, but no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Keeping your Android device updated and using appropriate device security features helps protect locally stored data.

12. Policy changes

This privacy policy may be updated when AnimePulse adds or removes features, changes data handling practices, or integrates new third-party SDKs. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the date of the most recent revision.

Significant changes will be noted in the AnimePulse app update release notes where practical.
